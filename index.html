The Hypothesis

Most teams reaching for AI right now make the same mistake. They bolt a chatbot on top of a framework that only exists in the founder's head. Six months later, the chatbot answers well but the framework has not moved.

We think about this differently. Sixty percent of what ChiKobi does every week is ordinary software: scheduling, content delivery, pathway tracking, reminders, intake. Thirty percent is rule-based logic: who qualifies for which pillar, which nudge fires when, which pathway branches where. Ten percent is the part that genuinely needs an AI layer: the personalized, culturally-grounded judgment that makes ROMY feel like a companion rather than a form.

The 8 Pillars, mapped to layers

One methodology. Three operational layers.

Ordinary Software (60%)

Screening intake, session scheduling, PreviCare pathway tracking, reminders, content delivery across Heart & Harmony, Balance & Bloom, Mind & Momentum.

Rule-Based Logic (30%)

Pillar qualification, nudge timing, pathway branching, referral triggers, escalation rules when a client falls off between sessions.

AI Layer (10%)

The culturally-grounded, personalized judgment that makes ROMY feel like a companion. Tuned to culture and lifestyle, the way a senior case manager would guide a junior one.

The 8 Pillars of Preventative Health™ is a ChiKobi Health trademark. The framework stays yours. The architecture around it is what this work would shape.
